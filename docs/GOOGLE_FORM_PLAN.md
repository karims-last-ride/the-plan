## How To Embed the Form

1. Open the Google Form.
2. Click `Send`.
3. Choose the embed icon.
4. Copy the iframe code.
5. Open `index.html`.
6. Find the `#vote` section.
7. Search for this comment:

```html
Mason: Replace this placeholder with the Google Form embed iframe.
```

8. Paste the iframe where the placeholder card currently lives.

## How To Update the Fallback Poll Button

In the same section, find:

```html
<a class="button button-primary" href="#" aria-label="Open Planning Poll placeholder">
  Open Planning Poll
</a>
```

Replace `href="#"` with the Google Form share URL.

## Sensitive Information Warning

Do not collect or publish overly sensitive information in the form. Avoid:

- Exact home addresses
- Payment account handles
- Personal phone numbers unless there is a clear private reason
- Government IDs or travel confirmation numbers
- Anything explicit, embarrassing, or incriminating

Final payment details should be handled privately in the group chat or direct
messages, not on this public website.
