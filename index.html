The Mission

A mountain weekend built around Karim.

Thursday evening through Sunday evening, the crew heads to a North Georgia cabin near Blue Ridge, Cherry Log, Ellijay, or wherever the right rental finally wins. Hot tub, fire pit, outdoor games, TVs, music, food, and one memorable off-site activity are the core.

The weekend should feel planned enough to be easy and loose enough to be fun. We are celebrating Karim, not building a minute-by-minute corporate retreat. The group votes first; Mason locks in the cabin, main activity, and cost split after the cutoff.

The Weekend Blueprint

Editable itinerary, no hidden build system.

The schedule is intentionally useful without pretending the weekend needs to be choreographed like a space launch.

Thu Aug 20

Arrival Night

  • Evening arrival window.
  • Unpack and claim rooms.
  • Easy dinner: pizza, wings, tacos, BBQ pickup, or whatever is practical.
  • First Karim toast.
  • Hot tub, games, music, and fire pit if available.
  • Keep Thursday as arrival/chill hype night, not the hardest drinking night.
Fri Aug 21

Cabin Day

  • Big breakfast.
  • Outdoor games, creek, forest, hot tub, and cabin hangout time.
  • Optional Blue Ridge / Ellijay outing, grocery restock, brewery, or chill reset.
  • Grill or steak dinner.
  • Karim roast, memory circle, trivia, or fake awards.
  • Fire pit and late-night cabin hangout.
Sat Aug 22

Main Event Day

  • Recovery breakfast.
  • Prep for rage room: closed-toe shoes, pants, clothes that can get dirty.
  • Drive to off-site activity with transportation planned carefully.
  • Smash N Dash Rage Room candidate.
  • Return to cabin.
  • Big bachelor dinner.
  • Main Karim toast and final big celebration night.
Sun Aug 23

Recovery + Sendoff

  • Slow breakfast.
  • Cleanup and cabin reset.
  • Final group photo.
  • Optional brunch or scenic stop if checkout is early.
  • Drive home Sunday evening if possible.
  • Late checkout should be requested, but not assumed.

Main Event Candidate

Smash N Dash Rage Room

Smash N Dash Rage Room in Resaca, Georgia is the current candidate for the big off-site activity: physical, funny, memorable, and chaotic without needing to be reckless.

Saturday afternoon is the preferred slot if the group chooses it. Book ahead, wear closed-toe shoes and pants, bring clothes you do not care about, and handle transportation like adults.

Important: do not combine drinking before the drive or activity. Plan sober driving carefully.

Cabin Requirements

The cabin has one job: make the weekend easy.

Must-have

  • Sleeps 7 comfortably
  • Hot tub
  • Big fire pit
  • 3+ bathrooms preferred
  • Large living room / group hangout area
  • TVs / Smart TVs
  • Kitchen
  • Grill
  • Outdoor space
  • Parking for multiple vehicles
  • Clear noise rules
  • AC
  • Reasonable drive to activity

Nice-to-have

  • Creek or forest access
  • Game room
  • Pool table
  • Foosball
  • Sauna
  • Mountain view
  • Covered deck
  • Bluetooth speakers
  • Outdoor fireplace
  • Pickleball, badminton, or cornhole area

Before booking, ask the host

  • Is the hot tub fully available for guest use?
  • Is there an outdoor fire pit or wood-burning fire area?
  • Are there quiet hours or outdoor music restrictions?
  • Is there enough parking for 3 vehicles or one larger rental vehicle plus cars?
  • Is the driveway accessible for a minivan or passenger van?
  • Are Bluetooth speakers or sound systems provided?
  • Is late checkout on Sunday possible?
